About Me

Hi! I’m Ernest, a self-motivated professional fueled by a relentless pursuit of knowledge, always on the lookout for ‘the coming thing’. My knack for strategic thinking and pattern recognition enables me to illuminate unseen connections, foresee the trajectories of challenges, and innovate solutions, often before problems materialize. My passion for continuous learning shapes my journey, with the thrill of the process as fulfilling as the outcomes. I am inspired by what the future could be, and I love energizing others with that vision. I’m a natural teacher and often find myself slipping comfortably into leadership roles, inspiring others to reach their full potential.

Outside of my professional interests I’m a musician, well-versed in audio engineering and production—the perfect intersection of art and science, of individuality and of the power of an ensemble. In my free time, I’m equally at home on a nature trail as I am in a cozy coffee shop, and you’re just as likely to find me with my nose buried in used record crates as you are in a good book.

At heart, I’m a relator who cherishes close relationships and finds immense satisfaction in working collaboratively towards shared goals. I approach life with an optimism that’s as constant as the North Star. Each day, I strive to boldly go where my curiosity leads me.
